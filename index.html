How It Works

The Circular Process

01

Collect & Deliver

Farmers bring harvested maize and crop residues to our local processing station for safe, efficient handling.

02

Shelling & Cleaning

Our mechanical shellers separate kernels from cobs quickly, reducing losses and protecting grain quality.

03

Feed Milling

We transform maize waste into nutrient-rich cattle, poultry, pig and goat feeds that are affordable and local.

Our Mission

Building Climate Resilience, One Community at a Time

We target smallholder farmers, farmer groups, and local markets across Kenya. By providing affordable maize shelling and locally milled livestock feed, we create a circular economy that benefits the entire agricultural value chain.

Our approach reduces dependence on imported feeds, minimizes post-harvest waste, and empowers communities to build sustainable livelihoods.
