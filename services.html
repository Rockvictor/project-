Our Services

From shelling to milling, we provide end-to-end agricultural processing to transform raw harvest into market-ready products and nutrient-rich livestock feed.

Maize Shelling

Fast, clean shelling that protects kernel quality and minimizes grain losses for your harvest.

Feed Milling

Transform cobs and stalks into balanced feed for poultry, dairy cows, pigs, and goats.

Poultry Feed

Locally milled feeds for layers and broilers with protein blends tuned for egg production and growth.

Dairy Feed

Balanced rations that support milk production and herd health using affordable maize-based ingredients.

Pig & Goat Feed

Energy-rich and fiber-balanced feeds for growing pigs, finishers, and goats at every stage.

Group Processing

Special support and pricing for farmer groups, cooperatives, and community batches.
